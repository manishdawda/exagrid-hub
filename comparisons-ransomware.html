Tiered air gap, delayed deletes, immutable objects, and AI anomaly detection.

Back to Categories

What Sets ExaGrid Apart

  • Tiered Backup Storage: Landing Zone (network-facing) + non-network-facing Repository Tier (tiered air gap).
  • Retention Time-Lock (RTL): policy-based delayed deletes + immutable deduplicated objects.
  • AI AutoDetect & Guard: detects anomalous delete patterns and extends delayed delete automatically.

Why It Matters

Delayed deletes preserve recovery points while immutability prevents modification—enabling reliable recovery even if delete commands are issued.
