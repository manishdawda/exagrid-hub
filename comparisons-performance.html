Performance & Restores

Keep recent backups undeduplicated for speed; deduplicate retention to control costs.

Back to Categories

Design Highlights

  • Recent backups reside in the Landing Zone (undeduplicated) for faster restore operations.
  • Long-term retention data is stored in the Repository Tier (deduplicated) to reduce storage footprint.

Operational Impact

Short RTOs for common restores while maintaining storage efficiency for aging backup data.
