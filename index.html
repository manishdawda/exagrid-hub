Why ExaGrid

  • Tiered Backup Storage architecture: Landing Zone (network-facing) + Repository Tier (non-network-facing, tiered air gap).
  • Retention Time-Lock (RTL): delayed deletes + immutable objects protecting backups from deletion/encryption.
  • AI-Powered RTL AutoDetect & Guard: detects anomalous delete patterns and extends delayed delete automatically.
